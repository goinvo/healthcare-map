US Healthcare System Map

Problem

There is no single view (diagram or schema) of the US healthcare system that describes the main actors and organizations, proces flows between those entities (care, decisions, rules, financial/funding), legal connections, or health access and outcomes impacts.

What we're doing

We're in the process of outlining, designing, and building an open source map of the US healthcare system.

Impact

We hope to not just shine a light on the structure of the US healthcare system, but as an open source tool, it can become a resource for academics, healthcare analysts, health IT engineers and designers, entrepreneurs finding their place in the system, and most of all for healthcare governance and policymakers
